EDA Charts?
1️.Histogram

Shows distribution of a single numeric column
Tells how values are spread
Used for: age, salary, marks

2️.Bar Chart
Compares categories
Used for: city count, gender count

3.Box Plot
Shows outliers
Shows minimum, maximum, median
Used for: salary range, marks variation

4️.Scatter Plot
Shows relationship between two numeric columns
Used for: age vs salary, height vs weight

5️.Heatmap
Shows correlation between columns
Used to check: which features are related

6️.Pie Chart
Shows percentage of categories
Used for: male/female ratio
